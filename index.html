Map of accidents

Accidents and injuries

Each dot represents a car crash or collision and the red dots are the accidents where one or more involved parties were injured

Key areas for accidents

This map shows you the different areas where this accident occurs. The different areas have been found using the K-means algorithm. The size of the circle represents how spread out the accidents are, where larger cirlce means the accidents are more spread out. The opacity shows you how many accidents there are so the more transparent the circle is the less accidents is happening in this area. Hover over the cirlces to see the area it covers and how many accidents is happening in the area. ...more explanation on this and how you can read maeanin from it
